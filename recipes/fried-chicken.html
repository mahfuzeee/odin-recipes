Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Buttermilk fried chicken that's incredibly tender, thanks to tangy buttermilk. After the buttermilk soak, dredge the chicken pieces in seasoned flour and fry them in hot oil until crisp and golden.

This buttermilk fried chicken recipe results in chicken thatâ€™s wonderfully crispy on the outside, but tender and juicy on the inside.

Ingredients

For the Chicken:

  • Whole chicken (cut into eight pieces)
  • Salt
  • Paprika
  • Dried rosemary
  • Dried sage
  • Buttermilk

For the seasoned flour:

  • All-purpose flour
  • Garilic powder
  • Onion powder

Steps

  1. Toss the chicken pieces in seasonings, then marinate in buttermilk overnight.
  2. Make the seasoned flour.
  3. Dredge each buttermilk-soaked chicken piece in flour and transfer to a plate.
  4. Fry the chicken until an instant read thermometer reads 165 degrees F.
  5. Drain on a cooling rack over a paper towel-lined baking sheet.
